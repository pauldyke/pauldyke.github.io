Email Developer

At first glance HTML for email appears to be a throwback to the 1990s with its use of tables and inline CSS, but there is more to it than that for a coder. Email platforms, such as Adestra's MessageFocus, allow the inclusion of dynamic content and templating which add an extra level of markup and logic. More advanced email clients and mobile devices can read CSS3 and media queries allowing for progressive enhancements, animations and responsive email design.
